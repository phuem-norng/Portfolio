Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you take on?

I specialize in UI/UX design for web and mobile applications, including user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design. I work with startups, small businesses, and agencies to create user-centered digital experiences.

What is your typical turnaround time?

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while a complex application could take several months. After discussing your project, I'll provide a detailed timeline.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients from all over the world. I'm comfortable with remote collaboration and have experience working across different time zones using various communication tools.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing, depending on the client's preference and project nature. After understanding your requirements, I'll provide a detailed quote with transparent pricing.
